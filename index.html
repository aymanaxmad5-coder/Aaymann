About Hormuud Telecom

Hormuud Telecom is the largest telecommunications company in Somalia. The company provides mobile communication, internet services, EVC Plus, and business solutions.

Founded in 2002, Hormuud Telecom serves millions of customers across Somalia and continues to improve digital communication and financial services.
